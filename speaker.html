Aaron Irizarry

DESINING A CULTURAL OF DESIGN

Workplace culture dosen`t start the beanbags, foosball tables, or a beer fridge, and it dosent end with neckties. pcs, or big corporation. its the unwritten rules, behavior, beliefs, and the motivations that enable good work get to done, or its what stifles a workforce. for design to the mosteffective and designers to feel valued, we need to work in a culture that embraces design and allow it to succeed.
In Aaron`s session he will explore how to recognize the traits of organization that *get* design, departements, and companies have that others don`t, and more importantly how to begin to change own Workplace culture. Once you`ve worked within a culture of design it`s almost impossible to imagine yourself anywhere else.
